Service & Scheduling

We respond to all inquiries within one business day, and most calls and emails are returned the same day. Once you request an estimate, we'll schedule the on-site visit within a few days and deliver your written quote within 24–48 hours of that visit. We understand that your time is valuable.

For interior work, we ask that you remove small valuables and fragile items from the work areas. We handle furniture — moving and covering it — as well as floor protection. For exterior work, you don't need to do much at all; we handle all the prep including pressure washing and surface repairs. We'll walk you through the specific prep steps during the estimate.

We do accommodate urgent situations on a case-by-case basis — for example, a property sale, tenant move-in, or storm damage repair. Call us directly at [Phone Number] to explain your situation and we'll do our best to find a solution within your timeline.

Before we consider any job complete, we walk through the finished work with you. We look at every surface together, address any touch-ups or concerns on the spot, and don't leave until you're fully satisfied. We also restore all furniture and leave the space cleaner than we found it. Your approval is the only milestone that closes the job.

Pricing & Estimates

We accept cash, check, and all major credit and debit cards. For larger projects, we typically collect a deposit at the start and the balance upon completion. Exact payment terms are outlined clearly in every written estimate we provide. Ask about financing options for qualifying projects.

Financing options may be available through third-party partners for qualifying projects. Contact us to discuss whether your project qualifies and what terms may be available. We want every customer to be able to get the quality results their home or business deserves, regardless of budget timing.

We stand behind all of our work with a workmanship guarantee. If any issue arises due to our application — peeling, cracking, or coverage inconsistencies — we'll return and correct it at no charge. The specific terms of the guarantee are included in every written contract. We want you to feel completely confident from the first brush to years down the road.
